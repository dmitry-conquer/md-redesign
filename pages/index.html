07 / Common questions

Before we
begin.

Clear answers about timing, platforms, SEO, mobile performance, and redesigns.

How long does it take to design a website?

Website design usually takes 4–12 weeks, depending on complexity. Simple sites need about 4–6 weeks, while advanced projects can take 8–12 weeks. Timing also depends on content and feedback.

Should I use a custom design or a website builder?

Custom designs provide stronger branding, SEO, scalability, performance, and conversion opportunities than generic website-builder templates.

What platforms do you work with?

Media Components primarily builds on WordPress and also works with Shopify, WooCommerce, and Magento, helping you choose the right platform for your goals.

Do you include SEO when building a website?

Basic SEO is included: fast load times, mobile-friendly design, clean code, and optimized meta tags. Full SEO services are also available.

Will my website work on mobile devices?

Yes. Every Media Components website is fully responsive, adapting to different screen sizes for fast loading and intuitive navigation.

Do you redesign existing websites?

Yes. The team analyzes the current site, modernizes its design, improves UX, and strengthens SEO and conversion performance around your new goals.
